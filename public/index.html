Catallactics and Intelligence

Mar 17, 2026

Intelligence is prediction. A model of what comes next, built from what came before. And what came before was evolutionary competition. Evolution lacked foresight. To remove a blind spot you need to make a creature blind for generations, each generation surviving and reproducing without sight, and rebuild the eye correctly using the same process that built it wrong. That’s what the evolutionary gamble of CNS solved. The central nervous system brought selection inside, simulating the outside world with available information from the sensory organs. The organism itself could now model, predict, and correct within a moment to bring about its vision for the world.

The Exoteric and Esoteric

Feb 16, 2025

The tension between esoteric and exoteric modes of communication shapes the very structure of how information operates. In systems governed by feedback and signal-processing —whether social, economic, or technological (which are largely the same thing, anyways) —the distinction between what is openly shared and what is strategically withheld becomes foundational. Exoteric is the reasoning behind mass media, democratic discourse, politics. The exoteric prioritizes visibility, ubiquity, and consensus. Exit is forbidden. It serves as the ideological infrastructure of politics, where legitimacy is tied to how widely an idea can be distributed and accepted. The exoteric demands participation, loyalty, presence—it feeds on noise and interprets silence as betrayal.
