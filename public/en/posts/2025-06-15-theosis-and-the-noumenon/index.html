Theosis and the Noumenon

KD
Jun 15, 2025

What is outside human sense is inferable by the effects it produces; it is disclosed through uncreated energy. Although it cannot be perceived, synergy with the noumenon is possible. Theosis is achieved through working with the emitted energies— the grace of God, that is, the mere presence of the unknowable, discernible in every part of the observable world. Synergy with God can only be confirmed by its accordance with the phenomena; this is what predictive ability entails. The greater one’s participation in uncreated energy, the closer to a god by grace, the clearer reality—and what is about to come—becomes.

That is why Alphanumeric Qabbala works.
